Comfort Cookies

Nutritious, Comforting Goodness

You can have your cake and eat it, too. Soft, gooey greatness without the guilt.

Ingridients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1 cup oats (optional)
  • 1 cup coconut oil, or butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1 cup unsweetened or dark chocolate chips for baking
  • Nutmeg, cinnamon, vanilla extract to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit and grease cookie sheet
  2. In a bowl, whisk together coconut oil or melted butter with maple syrup and vanilla
  3. Stir in flour, oats, nutmeg, and cinnamon. Then whisk in milk and egg
  4. Fold in chocolate chips as dough starts to form
  5. Use a spoon and your hands to create cookie shaped balls of dough. Place on cookie sheet and bake 25-35 minutes, depending on oven
